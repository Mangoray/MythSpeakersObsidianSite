##Kaile

In the Funeral Games, in order to honor the passing of Dintolneon, a relay race was one of the games part of the festival.
It tells the story of Kaile:

>Long ago, when bites of a miasmic Amphisbaena, a deadly creature, left her friend dying, the hero god Kaylee sought a cure from Cyclopean sages. They prophesied that only the sacred Tekolis herb hidden deep in the forest could purge the venom which could kill her friend three dawns hence.
>Though the forest was distant, Kaile wasted no time, racing the first dawn's sunlight as it spilled across the landscape. She climbed the morning mist as a shortcut, yet this drew the attention of her nemesis, the demon lord Nurgal.
>Hoping to stop her, he focused the sun's rays at her, burning the mist away. As she descended from the sky, Kaile hurled a javelin at the sun, wounding Nurgal, but not before the scorching sun set the islands ablaze.
>She raced on. Then, herb in hand, she seized a passing chariot and rushed back under the cover of night, the wildfires lighting her way.
>For her efforts, she arrived just before dawn, saved her friend, and inspired [[Pol-Bailax]] forever.

The Relay race involved a series of challenges, each telling a part of the story of Kaile:
- A shortcut through the mist -> runners must pass a herd of sheep.
- Nurgal attackin Kaile with the sun's rays -> runners must cross on hot coals.
