## Wavebreaker Clan
A [[PF2Data/Journals/PF2e Journals/Ancestries/Centaur|Centaur]] clan located on the [Iblydan archipelago](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Iblydos "Iblydos") city-state of [[Pol-Liachora]]. They are fierce coastal guardians who defended [[Liachora]] from the sea god [[Ousmariku ]] over a century ago. 

The clan thanks their name to being the vanguard during the battle against Ousmariku, 'breaking the waves' of the sea-borne behemoth.

[[Aiólos Wavebreaker]] comes from this clan, though exiled due to an incident where he found himself more interested in tracking a (presumably) legendary beast, abandoning his guard post. That slip-up ended in tragedy, costing the lives of some of his fellow clan members.
