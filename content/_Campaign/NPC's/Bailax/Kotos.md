## Kotos

_Male centaur ambassador_

The centaurs living in Bailax elect two tribunes, representing their people in the Philosopher Council. Kotos is one of these tribunes. He wants to make sure that the people he represents stick to the laws of the city, and occasionally asks adventurers to help him deal with rowdy and prank-performing centaurs.

Location: [[Bailax]]
