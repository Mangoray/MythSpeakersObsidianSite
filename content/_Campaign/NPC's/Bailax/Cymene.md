## Cymene

_Female centaur priest_ 

The pyres and flames of Bailax never extinguish, and keeping them ablaze in honor of Phyrtharros and his phoenix Salquin is part of Cymene’s duty. The centaur priest performs rituals in honor of Bailax’ pantheon, and she works closely with the cyclopes Myth-Speakers.
