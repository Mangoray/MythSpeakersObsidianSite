## Skorgomos

_Male cyclops fate-priest_ 

Leader of the cyclopean seers, Skorgomos and his fellow cyclopes perform the so-called myth-speaking for potential new hero-gods. By reading their fate, they can see what heroic deed one must perform to unlock their heroic potential, and start their path towards demi-godhood. However, successful Myth-Speakings have become rare, and Skorgomos worries like many other cyclopes in Iblydos that an age has come to an end.
