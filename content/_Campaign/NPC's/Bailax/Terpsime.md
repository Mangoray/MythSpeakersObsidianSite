## Terpsime

_Female human warrior-sage_ 
![[Terpsime.png|246x276]]
A former duelist and now member of Bailax’ Philosopher Council, Terpsime enjoys hosting visitors in her estate. She enjoys mingling with travelers and old friends alike. As a member of the Philosopher Council, she is involved in the leadership of the city-state.

Location: [[Bailax]]

_**Important**: The PCs begin this adventure as guests of Terpsime. **It is vital for you to have a reason to be her guest at the start of the adventure.**_
