## Ataiosiphon
_Male human potter_

A young and cheerful potter, Ataiosiphon is freshly engaged and excited about the prospect of marriage. His business is running well, and might expand in the near future!

Is engaged, but during a gathering in his house where his family-in-law were present, they were confronted by a Xenia Ghost.
