## Phel
![[Phel.png|Phel|250x250]]


_Nonbinary merfolk treasure hunter_

A so-called ‘wrecker’, Phel searches the cost of Bailax and nearby islands for wrecks and their treasures. They sell many of their findings in the [[Bailax]] markets, and enjoy trying new foods.

[[Aiólos Wavebreaker | Aiólos]] works for Phel as a deckhand and sometimes guides Phel as they descent into the treacherous waters of Iblydos as they dive for riches in the sunken shipwrecks.

Location: [[Bailax]]
