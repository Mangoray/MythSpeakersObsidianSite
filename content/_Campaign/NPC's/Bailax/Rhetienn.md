## Rhetienn

_Female centaur brewer_

Like many centaurs, Rhetienn lives a semi-nomadic life outside of Bailax. She does come regularly to the city though, selling her spirits made from fruits of the orchards she keeps. Rhetienn also has a reputation for playing hard, and partying even harder.

Location: [[Bailax]]
