## Sappil

_Male [[Character Building/Ancestries/Vishkanya|Vishkanya]] mercenary_

Sappil hails from Vudra, looking for military work here in Iblydos. He sends much of his earnings back home to support his family, and his serious demeanor belies a quick and wry sense of humor.
