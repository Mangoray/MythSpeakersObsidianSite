Also known as The Red Mantis, 'he who walks in blood'. Followers of certain Assassin guilds or Bounty Hunters sometimes do their work, not for coin, but for more religious reasons following Achaekek.

In a vision in [[Session04]], the party surmised that, after a vision both Echo and Aiólos experienced, the gash on [[Gorum|Gorum's]] armor was caused in a battle with [[Achaekek]].
