# Summary of Aiólos Wavebreaker
---

## Concept Overview

A young [[PF2Data/Journals/PF2e Journals/Ancestries/Centaur|Centaur]] [[PF2Data/Journals/PF2e Journals/Classes/Ranger|Ranger]] with the [[Beast Seeker]] background and the Outwit Hunter's Edge. The Wavebreaker clan guards coastal settlements/cities from sea born threats/monsters. When in coastal areas/cities, a [[Feathered Herald | Common Shag]] (type of cormorant) circles around like a living omen, possibly guiding Aiólos toward mythic destiny.

---
# Appearance
From the waist up, he is lean and athletic, with sun-bronzed skin and a build built for speed. His dark-brown hair is shaven close on the sides, contrasting sharply with dreadlocks gathered into a practical ponytail atop his head.  He has high cheekbones and a strong jawline. Deep, warm brown eyes with a hint of gold.

Below the waist, the body of a Buckskin Bay horse flowed seamlessly from his human torso. A golden coat flowing into darkened to black on his strong legs.

## Roleplaying Hooks

- **Wavebreaker vanguard**  
    Aiólos hails from the [[Wavebreaker Clan | Wavebreaker]] centaur clan, coastal guardians who defended Liachora from the sea behemoth Ousmariku over a century ago.

- **Banishment**
	Aiólos was exiled from his clan following a tragic incident which cost the lives of his fellow clansmen. To the outside world, he proclaims he was chosen to do retrieve the spear of Psomeira. This makes him act (over)confident in many of the situations he finds himself in. While in reality he uses his 'chosen destiny' as an armor, or mask if you will, to hide his guilt about what happened.

- **Arrival in [[Bailax]]**
	After a couple of months sailing across the Kardaji Bay looking for any idea on how about to go on his task, he ends up in [[Bailax]]. Trying to make ends meet, and perhaps to get some lead about Ousmariku, he joins [[Phel]] on their treasure hunt as a deck hand and to offer them aid with his knowledge on (sea) creatures and protection when the hunts for loot get a bit dicey.
	
	After an especially rare haul of artifacts which caught the eye of an attendant of [[Terpsime]]. The artifacts to be delivered, Phel mentioned to Terpsime Aiólos' boisterous tale of 'Retrieving the spear that drove off Ousmariku'.

- **[[Feathered Herald]]**  
	 During his fishing, Aiólos started out just tossing the bird some fish now and then, more out of curiosity than anything. But after the bird scared off a Sedacthy -screeching and spreading its huge wings to protect Aiólos-  wherever Aiólos travels, the Common Shag is never far away.
