## **Iu'yuan**  
_Songbird Strix Polymath Bard (Fledgling Flight)_

- **Appearance:** Steely blue back/wings, rufous chest, cinnamon forehead/throat feathers; practical linen blouse/skirt, golden ankle bangles, silk scarf (doubles as headscarf). Always has a manuscript brush behind her ear.
- **Personality:** Thoughtful, cheerful, clever, combat-averse (prefers diplomacy), loves books/fish, _hates_ poultry.
- **Key Ties:**  
    → **[[Mei]]:** Bonds over hero-god stories (Mei’s past lives).  
    → **[[Echo]]:** Trades trinkets; finds her "useful but untrustworthy."  
    → **[[Phel]]:** Playful rivalry in trinket hunting; shares food skewers (tracks Phel’s culinary experiments).  
    → **[[Terpsime]]:** Workplace respect (Philosopher’s Council colleague).  
    → **Rack’naw:** Harpy foe (robbed her on boat trip; still unsettles her).  
    → **[[Sappil]]:** Vudran ally (shares Jalmaray heritage, appreciates his wit).
- **Locations:**  
    → **Acropolis:** Scribes religious texts for Philosopher’s Council (stable but tedious job).  
    → **Kopis Heights:** Pro-bono letter writing for shrines/domestic needs.  
    → **Cloudcrag Aerie (Jalmaray cliffs):** Birthplace; home to Tian diaspora.  
    → **Pyre-fowl nests:** Visits with trinket gifts for mated harpies.
- **Quirks:** Magpie for trinkets (Salquin’s holiday scavenges), loves reading hands-free in bed, _never_ eats poultry.
