[**Ousmariku**]() is a [thalassic behemoth](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Thalassic_behemoth "Thalassic behemoth") who annually terrorizes the city of [[Aelyosos]] for tribute. It also destroyed the [Iblydan](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Iblydos "Iblydos") city of [Liachora](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Liachora "Liachora") at the dawn of the [Age of Lost Omens](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Age_of_Lost_Omens "Age of Lost Omens").[1](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Ousmariku#cite_note-distant-shores-8--1)

## Personal Quest
[[Aiólos Wavebreaker]] Set out from his centaur clan to 'retrieve the spear of Psomeira' as proof to become a Hero-God 
In reality, he was expelled from the wandering tribe due to an incident that caused the death of fellow clan members.

Trying to make amends, he forced it upon himself to retrieve the spear, in order to retore his and his families honor.

## History

Ousmariku was summoned by the unified cries of [cyclopes](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Cyclops "Cyclops") in [Pol-Liachora](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Pol-Liachora "Pol-Liachora") after the death of [Aroden](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Aroden "Aroden") in [4606 AR](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/4606_AR "4606 AR"). The cyclopes proclaimed that a primordial beast would punish their people for long-forgotten crimes until the guilty parties were brought to justice.

Months later, Ousmariku ate Liachora's navy. Iblydos rallied its ten greatest [hero-gods](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Hero-god "Hero-god") to oppose the [behemoth](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Behemoth "Behemoth") and protect Liachora, but the behemoth killed them all and destroyed the city. Only the [spear](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Spear "Spear") of [Psomeira](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Psomeira "Psomeira"), one of Liachora's guards, harmed Ousmariku enough to repel it, but not before it decimated Psomeira's forces and apparently killed her.

Liachora's people fled to Aelyosos as refugees, and Ousmariku returned to Aelyosos annually to demand tribute. Psomeira, however, reappeared in Aelyosos a decade later as a hero-god, where she has since served as Aelyosos' co-ruler and defender, and has drilled the city's defenders for an eventual final battle against the behemoth. Until then, the city has continued to pay its annual tribute to Ousmariku.
