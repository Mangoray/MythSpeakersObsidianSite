## Aelyosos
![[Aelyosos.jpg]]
**[Aelyosos](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Aelyosos)** is the westernmost city of note in the archipelago of [Iblydos](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Iblydos "Iblydos") in the [Obari Ocean](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Obari_Ocean "Obari Ocean"). It serves as the gateway to Iblydan culture and hospitality to most travelers on the [Obari Crossing](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Obari_Crossing "Obari Crossing"), and due to its many gardens and the tireless work of its citizens, is an overall peaceful place to rest and conduct trade.

## Government

Aelyosos is the inheritor-capital city of [[Pol-Liachora]] filling the role since the destruction of its sister city and former capital [Liachora](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Liachora "Liachora") by the [thalassic behemoth](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Thalassic_behemoth "Thalassic behemoth") [Ousmariku](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Ousmariku "Ousmariku") during the dawn of the [Age of Lost Omens](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Age_of_Lost_Omens "Age of Lost Omens").Both are ruled by the absolute authority of the hero-gods [Psomeira](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Psomeira "Psomeira") and [Kelksiomides](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Kelksiomides "Kelksiomides"), who are advised on matters by [Chief Myth-Speaker Phimater](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Phimater "Phimater") and the [oceanid](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Oceanid "Oceanid") aristocrat [Sjoweir](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Sjoweir "Sjoweir"), as well as by a [non-binding council](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Council_\(Aelyosos\) "Council (Aelyosos)") of local representatives.
