Per scholars of [Iblydan](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Iblydos "Iblydos") history, **Pol-Liachora** is one of the archipelago's nine great historical city-states. Its inheritor-capital is [Aelyosos](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Aelyosos "Aelyosos"), filling the role since the destruction of its sister city and former capital [[Liachora]] by the [thalassic behemoth](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Thalassic_behemoth "Thalassic behemoth") [Ousmariku](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Ousmariku "Ousmariku") during the dawn of the [Age of Lost Omens](https://pathfinderwiki.com/wiki/Age_of_Lost_Omens "Age of Lost Omens").
