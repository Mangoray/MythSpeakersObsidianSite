## Session 01 The Funeral Games

- Approx. 4000 years in the past, under a darkened sky, we see some kind of infrastructure, hovering over a roiling ocean.
	- People walk on it, rain hitting their faces. however we do not see their faces. Clad in robes and/or armor. They have small devices on them.
	- Conversations cannot be made up. But one of them points out toward the ocean, a titanic Figure emerges from the surface of the water.
	- It tips over the platform, people falling/disappearing into the maw of the creature.

#### Toilsday, 2nd of Erastus, 4725
- [[Nawel]] is introduced. waking up in[[Terpsime | Terpsime's]] home. An automaton with a panther like face, light blue glowing eyes. Close to him lies a sword and shield.
- We move to a small dueling chamber, [[Mei]] is introduced. A Leshy, child height, her head's hair has a flow shaped like a peach. She's using a large axe. Mei's shows off her healing skills.
- The scene switches to a storage room where [[Echo]] is found. Filled with food. Her eye catches a casket of wine bottles. dark purple colored with a swirling of 'galaxy' in it. She (tries to) swipe a bottle. an attendant notices Echo and leads her out of the storage room.
- In a large sitting area looking over the Bailax bay [[Iu'yuan]] is introduced. A Strix Bard Scribbling furiously, she's interrupted by Aiólos
- [[Aiólos Wavebreaker]] is introduced, carrying a big box with artifacts. they are joined by Nawel, Echo and Mei.

- [[Terpsime]] enters the room and invites the party to dinner. Everyone is seated at a large table which is covered in delicious food. She explains of Dintolneon the hero-god's passing. 
	- A hero, but also a protector of the city. He had quite the history of saving the city from various dangers. In one such instance he once, barehanded, bested a manticore.
- She wants to 'field a team' a spot in the funeral games, as a chance to enjoy the competitive games, to get to know the city and the people. to honor Dintolneon, and maybe win some prizes.
- Visit the cyclops Undyl to finish the application.
- [[Iu'yuan]] knows some gossip that [[Ataiosiphon]] already has put together a team.
- The party heads to 'The Kylix' ward of the city. Iconography and banners of the Phoenix are displayed around the city.
	- A Pyrefowl crosses their path.
	- Aiólos picks up a feather, which still has some embers at the end.
	- [[Iu'yuan]] hands some rations to the bird.
- [[Mellita]] approaches Mei, and asks about the signing up.
- [[Sappil]] approaches the party and also talks about signing up. Makes a joke about Mei's height.
- [[Phel]] interjects with some banter.
- They enter the building and Cyclops [[Undyl]] hands them a form to sign up for the Funeral Games.
- Aiólos has a conversation with his Shag, hoping that it'll lure some fishes in the way of the party during the games.
- Snake headed person with green eyes and a lyre on his back. Looks up at the sky, and we see the tiniest speck of fire.
