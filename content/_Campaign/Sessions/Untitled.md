### Session Flow
1. **Opening Scene**:  
   - Automaton Nawel wakes in Terpsime's home (sword/shield nearby).  
   - Mei demonstrates healing skills in dueling chamber.  
   - Echo tries to steal wine in storage room (caught by attendant).

2. **Terpsime's Invitation**:  
   - Hosts dinner, explains Dintolneon's passing (hero who defeated manticore barehanded).  
   - Proposes "Funeral Games" to honor him + win prizes.  
   - **Critical Task**: Visit Cyclops Undyl to apply.

3. **Team Formation**:  
   - Party joins Terpsime's team: *Mei (captain), Nawel (fisher), Aiólos (captain), Iu'yuan (fisher)*.  
   - Rival team *Golden Pegasi* (led by Ataiosiphon):  
     - Yphani (cyclops herder), Rhetienn (centaur brewer), Mellita (weaver), Sappil (Vishkanya), Phel.

4. **Game Event**: Competitive Sardine Fishing  
   - **Party's Team**:  
     - Mei/Nawel: Captured 9 bulk (giant golden sardine + 2 more)  
     - Aiólos/Iu'yuan: Only 4 bulk (struggles catching fish)  
   - **Rival Team**: Akki (Tengu) interrupts with Team Akki (Human Tsemone + lizard folk).

### Key Lore Notes
- **Aiólos' Backstory**:  
  Exiled from Wavebreaker clan after failing to defend Liachora from Ousmariku (not "retrieving a spear"). His "chosen destiny" lie masks guilt.  
  *Recommended Fix*: He should **acknowledge his failure** and **join Wavebreakers' next defense** (not boast).
  
- **Feathered Herald**:  
  Common Shag (mythic omen) circles Aiólos. Should symbolize his *growth* (not superstition).
  
- **Terpsime's Rule**:  
  **PCs must have a valid reason to be her guest** (e.g., artifacts from Phel, debt repayment, or connection to her council).
