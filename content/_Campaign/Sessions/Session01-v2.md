### Session Flow
1. **Opening Scene**:  
- Approx. 4000 years in the past, under a darkened sky, we see some kind of infrastructure, hovering over a roiling ocean.
	- People walk on it, rain hitting their faces. however we do not see their faces. Clad in robes and/or armor. They have small devices on them.
	- Conversations cannot be made up. But one of them points out toward the ocean, a titanic Figure emerges from the surface of the water.
	- It tips over the platform, people falling/disappearing into the maw of the creature.
- Present day, Toilday, 2nd of Erastus 
   - Automaton Nawel wakes in Terpsime's home (sword/shield nearby).  
   - Mei demonstrates her fighting, and self-healing skills in dueling chamber.  
   - Echo tries to steal wine in storage room (caught by attendant).

2. **Terpsime's Invitation**:  
   - Hosts dinner, explains Dintolneon's passing (hero who defeated manticore barehanded).  
   - Proposes "Funeral Games" to honor him + win prizes.  
   - **Critical Task**: Visit Cyclops Undyl to apply.

3. **Team Formation**:  
   - Party joins Terpsime's team: *Mei (captain), Nawel (fisher), Aiólos (captain), Iu'yuan (fisher)*.  
	   - Echo stays on shore.
   - Rival team *Golden Pegasi* (led by Ataiosiphon):  
     - [[Yphani]] (cyclops herder), [[Rhetienn]] (centaur brewer), [[Mellita]] (weaver), [[Sappil]] (Vishkanya), [[Phel]].
 - Snake headed person with green eyes, snake-like eyes, and a lyre on his back. Looks up at the sky, and we (the audience) see the tiniest speck of fire far, far away.

The Next Day, on Wealday, the 3rd of Erastus

3. **Game Event**: Competitive Sardine Fishing
   - **Party's Team**:  
     - Mei/Nawel: Captured 9 bulk (giant golden sardine + 2 more)  
     - Aiólos/Iu'yuan: Only 4 bulk (struggling catching fish)
     - Echo sabotages the catch of the rival teams. Also starts a pool to see who will win.
   - **Rival Team**: Akki (Tengu) interrupts with Team Akki (Human Tsemone + lizard folk).
   - **Critical Task**: play the funeral games!
